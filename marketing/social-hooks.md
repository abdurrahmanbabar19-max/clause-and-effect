# Clause & Effect — 10 ready-to-post hooks & captions

How to use: the **hook** is the first line / on-screen text (the scroll-stopper).
The **caption** goes underneath. End every post with the same CTA so the algorithm
learns what you want people to do. Keep the compliance line in your bio, not every post.

CTA to reuse: 👉 Free AI contract check (link in bio) — first 50 people, on the house.

---

## 1. The "you got robbed" hook  (TikTok / Reel — screen-record the AI reviewer)

### Posting tips
- Post the **video** hooks (1, 4, 5, 7, 8) on TikTok + Reels — those travel furthest.
- Post the **carousels** (2, 6) on Instagram.
- Repurpose: every video can become a carousel and vice-versa.
- Reply to every comment in the first hour — it boosts reach.
- Pin your best-performing post and put the link in your bio.
