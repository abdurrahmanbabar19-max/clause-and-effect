FAQ

Is this legal advice?

No — and we're upfront about that. Clause & Effect is a plain-English explainer service that helps you understand a contract and spot issues. We don't provide regulated legal advice or represent you. For anything high-stakes or complex, we'll tell you clearly and point you to a qualified solicitor.

How fast will I get my report?

Standard turnaround is 48 hours from when you send the contract. Members and urgent requests can get it within 24 hours.

Is my contract kept private?

Completely. Your documents are confidential, used only to prepare your report, and never shared. You can ask us to delete them once you've got your report.

What types of contracts do you check?

Freelance and client agreements, brand/influencer deals, NDAs, tenancy agreements, supplier and co-founder agreements, and most everyday contracts. If it's outside what we can responsibly help with, we'll tell you before charging anything.

What if I need changes made to the contract?

The Deep Read includes a negotiation checklist and ready-to-send wording so you can request changes yourself. We don't draft binding documents for you, but we make asking for what you want simple.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes. Students and first-time founders get their first contract check free — just mention it in your request.
